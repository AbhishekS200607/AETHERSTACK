10 Questions Clients Ask Us Before Hiring — Answered Honestly

Aetherstack Team · 6 min read

We get a lot of questions before clients decide to work with us. That's a good thing — it means they're thinking carefully. Here are the 10 most common ones, answered as honestly as we can.

Question 1

"Are you students? Can I trust you?"

Yes, we are students. That means we charge less, work harder to prove ourselves, and actually care about your project. We don't take your money and disappear — our name and domain are attached to this. Every project we deliver is part of our portfolio. We have every reason to do good work.

Question 2

"What if I don't like the design?"

2 free revision rounds are included in every plan. We don't lock you into the first version. Before we start, we ask you to share examples of websites you like — so we're aligned from day one, not guessing.

Question 3

"How do I know you'll finish on time?"

We agree on a deadline before work starts and put it in writing. You pay 50% after delivery — so we have every reason to finish on time. We've never missed a committed deadline.

Question 4

"Do I need to know anything technical?"

Nothing at all. You tell us what your business does, what you want on the site, and share any photos or text you have. We handle everything else — design, development, hosting setup, domain configuration. You just review and approve.

Question 5

"What if the website breaks after delivery?"

Free bug fixes for 15 days after delivery — no questions asked. After that, our ₹500/month maintenance plan covers everything: updates, fixes, small changes, and monitoring. You're never left stranded.

Question 6

"Is ₹1,000 really enough for a good website?"

For a single landing page with a contact form and WhatsApp button — yes, absolutely. It won't have 20 pages and a payment system, but it will be clean, fast, mobile-responsive, and professional. We don't cut corners on quality regardless of the plan size.

Question 7

"Will my website appear on Google?"

Basic SEO is included in every plan — proper page titles, meta descriptions, and Google indexing submission. Full SEO setup with keyword optimization and Google Search Console configuration is ₹500 extra. Results typically take 4–12 weeks to show, depending on your location and competition.

Question 8

"Can you copy a website I like?"

We take inspiration from sites you like and build something original in that direction. We don't copy code — your site is built fresh. This protects you legally and means your site is actually optimized for your content, not someone else's.

Question 9

"What do you need from me to start?"

Four things: your business name, what you want on the site, any images or text you already have, and 50% advance payment. That's it. If you don't have images, we can use professional stock photos. If you don't have text, we'll write it based on a short conversation with you.

Question 10

"What if I want to add more features later?"

No problem at all. We can add features anytime after delivery. Additional pages cost ₹300 each. Bigger features like a login system, payment gateway, or admin panel are quoted separately based on scope. Your site grows as your business grows.

Still have a question that's not on this list? Send us a message — we'll answer it the same way we answered these. Honestly.
