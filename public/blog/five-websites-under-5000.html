5 Types of Businesses in Kerala That Need a Website Under ₹5,000

Aetherstack Team · 4 min read

Most small business owners in Kerala think a website is something only big companies need. Or that it costs a lot of money. Or that it's complicated to manage.

None of that is true anymore.

A simple, well-built website costs less than a month's worth of flyers — and it works 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, for years. Here are 5 types of businesses where a website under ₹5,000 makes an immediate, measurable difference.

school

Type 1

Tuition Centers and Home Tutors

Parents search for tutors online now. A WhatsApp forward only reaches people who already know you. But a parent in your area searching "maths tutor for Class 10 Kochi" on Google can find you — if you have a website.

A simple page with your subjects, experience, batch timings, fees, and a WhatsApp button gets you more student inquiries than any flyer or notice board.

Recommended plan: ₹1,000 – ₹2,500
storefront

Type 2

Local Shops and Retailers

Even if you don't sell online, a page showing your products, location, and timings puts you on Google Maps and local search. When someone searches "hardware shop near Palakkad" — you appear.

It also gives you a link to share on WhatsApp groups, which is how most local business referrals happen in Kerala. Instead of typing out your address every time, you send a link.

Recommended plan: ₹1,000 – ₹2,500
photo_camera

Type 3

Freelance Photographers and Videographers

Your Instagram shows your work. Your website shows your professionalism. These are two different things, and clients who want to pay serious money check both.

A portfolio website with your best work, your packages, and a contact form positions you as a professional — not just someone with a camera. It's the difference between getting ₹5,000 for a wedding shoot and getting ₹25,000.

Recommended plan: ₹2,500 – ₹4,500
restaurant

Type 4

Home-Based Food Businesses

Homemade pickles, cakes, tiffin services, Kerala snacks — these businesses grow entirely through trust and word of mouth. A website accelerates both.

A simple page with your menu, prices, photos of your food, and an "Order via WhatsApp" button turns curious visitors into paying customers. It also makes you look established — not just a home cook, but a real food business.

Recommended plan: ₹1,000 – ₹2,500
medical_services

Type 5

Clinics and Independent Practitioners

Doctors, physiotherapists, dieticians, counselors, Ayurveda practitioners — patients Google before they call. Always. A professional-looking site with your qualifications, specialization, clinic timings, and contact builds immediate trust.

In healthcare, trust is everything. A website that looks credible is the difference between a patient choosing you or choosing the clinic down the road that has one.

Recommended plan: ₹2,500 – ₹4,500

All five types have one thing in common: their customers are already searching online. The only question is whether they find you — or your competitor.
