groups About the Team

Who is AetherStack? expand_more

A team of B.Tech Computer Science students from Kerala who build websites for small businesses and startups at affordable prices. We combine fresh academic knowledge with practical engineering to deliver high-quality web solutions.

Are you a registered company? expand_more

We are a student-founded agency. While not formally registered as a private limited company yet, we operate professionally with proper payment terms, legal contracts, and documented delivery processes.

How do I know you won't disappear with my money? expand_more

Transparency is our core value. We take only a 50% advance to start work. The remaining payment is made only after you have reviewed and approved the final delivery. We have a real domain, real professional emails, and a verified team of students with reputations to maintain.

assignment About Projects

What kind of websites do you build? expand_more

We specialize in Frontend websites, full stack web apps (Node.js or Java Spring Boot), landing pages, portfolios, e-commerce stores, and custom SaaS platforms tailored to your specific business needs.

Do you build mobile apps? expand_more

Currently, we focus exclusively on web development to ensure the highest quality in that domain. We do not build native Android or iOS apps at this time.

Can you redesign my existing website? expand_more

Absolutely. We can take your existing website and completely redesign it with a modern aesthetic and rebuild the backend for better performance and security.

How long does a project take? expand_more

Timeline varies by complexity:
• Frontend: 3–15 days
• Node.js Full Stack: 7–30 days
• Spring Boot Full Stack: 10–40 days

payments About Pricing

How much does a website cost? expand_more

Starting from ₹1,000 for basic frontend sites. Full stack solutions start at ₹3,000. For a more accurate estimate, we recommend using our Cost Calculator.

Is hosting and domain included? expand_more

No. Hosting and domain are separate ongoing costs paid to third-party providers. However, we can handle the entire setup for you as an add-on service.

What payment methods do you accept? expand_more

We accept all major UPI apps (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), as well as direct Bank Transfers.
