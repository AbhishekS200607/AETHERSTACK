The Lifecycle

From Idea
to Launch.

A clear, simple process so you always know what's happening with your project. No black boxes, just pure execution.

1

You Reach Out

Fill our contact form or WhatsApp us directly. Tell us what you need — even a rough idea is enough to get started.

2

Free Discussion Call

We schedule a quick 30-minute call or WhatsApp chat to understand your requirements, goals, and budget. No commitment needed.

3

Quote and Timeline

Within 24 hours we send you a detailed quote with exact price, what's included, and how long it will take.

4

Advance Payment

Once you approve the quote, 50% advance payment is made via UPI or bank transfer. This is when work officially begins.

5

Design and Development

We start building. You'll receive progress updates regularly via WhatsApp so you're never left wondering what's happening.

6

First Preview

We share a working preview link for you to review. You give feedback on what to change or improve.

7

Revisions

We make changes based on your feedback. 2 free revision rounds are included in all plans.

8

Final Delivery

Final version is delivered. Remaining payment is made. Source code and all files are handed over to you.

9

Post Delivery Support

Free bug fixes for 15 days after delivery. Monthly maintenance available at ₹500/month if needed.
