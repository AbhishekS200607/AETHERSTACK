Mini FAQ

Do you provide hosting and domain? expand_more

No, but we can set it up for you as an add-on service. Hosting typically costs ₹0–₹500/month depending on the platform (many small sites can run on free tiers).

How do we communicate during the project? expand_more

Directly via WhatsApp or email. You'll always talk to the developer working on your project, not a middleman or account manager.

Can I request changes after delivery? expand_more

Yes, 2 free revision rounds are included in all plans. Additional changes or new features after the 15-day bug-fix period are billed at ₹300 per change.

What if I don't know what I want exactly? expand_more

No problem. Fill the contact form and we'll guide you through everything in a free 30-minute discussion call to map out your needs.

Do you work with clients outside Kerala? expand_more

Yes, we work fully remote across India. All communication is online through WhatsApp, Email, and Google Meet.
